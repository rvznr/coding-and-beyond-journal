About Us

Your trusted source for insights into technology's impact on our world.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between tech enthusiasts and experts by delivering insightful, well-researched, and accessible content. Empowering individuals through knowledge, we strive to drive innovation and foster a sustainable future.

Our Vision

To become a leading voice in technology journalism by inspiring conversations about the transformative power of innovation, ethics, and responsibility in the digital age.

Our History

Coding and Beyond was conceived in early 2023, inspired by the rapidly evolving intersection of technology and society. Our foundation is rooted in collaboration, with OpenAI's assistant providing guidance on tone, structure, and research focus. Though speculative in parts, our story represents a shared passion for progress and learning.
